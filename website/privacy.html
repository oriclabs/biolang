Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

BLViewer is a browser-based bioinformatics file viewer built by Oric Labs. This policy covers the BLViewer Chrome extension and the web version at lang.bio/viewer.

Data Collection

BLViewer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No cookies (other than local theme preference)
  • No telemetry or usage reporting
  • No account or login required
  • No data is sent to any server

How Your Files Are Processed

All file parsing and viewing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly. Your files never leave your device. No data is uploaded, cached on a server, or shared with any third party.

Local Storage

BLViewer uses your browser's local storage solely to remember:

  • Your theme preference (light or dark)
  • A list of recently opened file names (names only, not file contents)

This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

BLViewer does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
